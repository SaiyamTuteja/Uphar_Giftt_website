About Us

We are team member!
we are the students of Bachelor's students in COER University, Roorkee, Uttrakhand.!

There is nothing better than seeing your loved ones smile wide!
A gift might just do that. Whether it be someone's birthday, wedding anniversary, or showering your love by giving them unexpected small gifts in a daily ordinary life.
That's exactly what led to the birth of this venture -UPHAAR GIFT GALLERY.
We help you gift your loved ones something unique, handmade and something that express your feelings.
All of these gifts are tailor made for you and of course handmade in my small little working space. We showcase our art on Instagram and Pinterest.
If interested we can be contacted via Instagram DMs and e-mail.
OUR MOTTO
We believe that every day is an occasion to make our loved ones smile.
And handmade gifts are unique and personal enough to show your love and effort.
Despite that, we know not it's not that easy or quick and we can't always execute it while keeping it a secret.
We want to make this process of making handmade gifts easy and fast by providing you with our products that can be filled with your messages, pictures and love later.
